About

Elecwire

With over 20 years experience in the industry,
we have worked with both large and small customers and pride ourselves on straightforward pricing and quality workmanship.
Elecwire was founded in 2004 and is a full-service in commercial and residential.

Working with us is convenient and straightforward.
Whatever the job you have in mind, we'll draw upon our experience to find a solution that minimizes disruption and suits the needs of your business.

01

Electrical
Construction

We can install or refurbish a wide range of electrical systems so that they comply with current safety standards. All our installations are arranged to cause minimal disruption to your business and, where possible, can be completed outside of business hours.

02

Emergency
Services

Our emergency electrical services are available 24/7 to address critical issues like power outages, electrical failures, and hazardous situations. Our rapid response team will quickly diagnose and resolve the problem to minimize downtime.

03

Maintenance
& Repair

Our comprehensive electrical maintenance programs ensure your systems remain in optimal condition. Regular inspections, preventive repairs, and timely replacements help avoid costly breakdowns and extend the lifespan of your electrical infrastructure.

04

Smart Home
Integration

Transform your space with our advanced smart home and building automation solutions. We integrate lighting, climate control, security systems, and appliances into a seamless, energy-efficient network that you can control from anywhere.
